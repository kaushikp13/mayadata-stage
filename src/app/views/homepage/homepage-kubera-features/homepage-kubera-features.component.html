Unleash Kubernetes as your data plane

Per workload Container Attached Storage - including access to the underlying hardware & cloud volumes via LocalPV - supports the loose coupling of workloads and teams that Kubernetes enables.

The skills, tools, and best practices of managing Kubernetes apply to the use of it as a data plane. By comparison the care and feeding of shared everything storage services and hardware systems is a specialized skill, leading to team silos and lock-in.

Save Money with Kubera

Using local disk or cloud volumes without Kubera can be extremely wasteful. Kubera uses the dynamic and often thin provisioning of OpenEBS to save 75% or more on cloud costs.

Kubera gives operators the visibility, reporting, alerting and more necessary to optimize capacity and also to automate the care and feeding of their environment, including upgrading the infrastructure and the stateful workloads running on Kubera, and configuring the environment for resilience via cross AZ configurations, back-ups, DR, or cloud migrations.
