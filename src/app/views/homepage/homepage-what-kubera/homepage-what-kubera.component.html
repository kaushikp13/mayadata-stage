What is Kubera?

Kubera provides storage for Kubernetes. Kubera includes a long term support version of OpenEBS, purpose built management software with an intuitive GUI and APIs, and other tools backed by 24x7 enterprise support.

The most popular open source, cloud native storage solution.

The benefits of Kubera

Kubera brings easy automation, enterprise level access controls, and push-button experiences to add resilience to data workloads on Kubernetes. Built and supported 24x7 by one of the leading contributors to CNCF projects - Kubera helps you to get all the benefits of running data on Kubernetes - without the operational headaches.
