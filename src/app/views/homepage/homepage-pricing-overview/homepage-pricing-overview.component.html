Pricing overview

Kubera comes in 3 different packages, based on common needs when running data on Kubernetes.

Each package comes with support for OpenEBS , which itself includes the broadest variety of storage engines available.

The basic package of Kubera is free forever and includes back-ups, visualization, off-cluster logging & monitoring, and more.

The team and enterprise versions include SLA based enterprise support for OpenEBS and related capabilities, governance reporting and alerts, simplified multi-cloud disaster recovery, enterprise integrations and access controls, on premise deployments, and more.

Check out the features of each package of Kubera and choose the best option for your Kubernetes environments, including EKS, AKS, OpenShift, Rancher, upstream Kubernetes and more.
