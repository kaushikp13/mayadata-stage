Meet Kubera

Kubera simplifies the operations of Kubernetes as your data plane

Having built the leading cloud native open source storage solution, OpenEBS, the team at MayaData has turned its attention to the operations of Kubernetes as a data plane. Kubera capabilities include:

  • Off-Cluster Logging
  • Alerting
  • Visualization
  • Reporting
  • Backups
  • Maintenance
  • Compliance checks
  • Troubleshooting
  • Lifecycle automation
